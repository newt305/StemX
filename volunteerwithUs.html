What you can do

STEMx has volunteers from high schools, colleges, and universities around the world earning community service hours! There are no deadlines for these positions, simply apply and we will reach out through email asap all year around. Note that you will need a discord account to be able to volunteer; it is our main form of communication.

Positions Open:

Writer:

Writers write 2-4 Instagram posts a month on a large variety of STEM-related topics. Almost always, we provide subtitles, instructions, and online sources so you can work on writing. We provide topics for you to choose from or you can subject topics that interest you! Please take a look at our Instagram (@stem__x) to gain an idea.

Editor:

Comment feedback on writers' work including grammar, creativity, wording, spelling, etc.

Social Media Manager:

Social Media Managers check on our Instagram about 2 times a week to manage DMs, shout-outs, and comments.

Please Note that this document covering STEMx's rules and procedure for social media access is required to be signed and uploaded to the application.
