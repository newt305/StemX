Interviews

Please only complete this form if what you do is related to STEM, mental health advocacy, or any other advocacy somehow related to STEM.

All your answers will be posted word by word. You can answer first person or third person.

Please be mindful of the length of your responses and caption, but do not put in one-word responses.

We do NOT do interviews for organizations and ONLY individuals, but your organization will be part of the main idea.
