Welcome To STEMx

A global initiative dedicated to STEM education

What We Do

STEMx is a global youth-led initiative led by Aryana Motlagh founded in 2020 with a mission to spread STEM education, currently with over 1.3k followers and about 20 volunteers. We post informational topics on Instagram spreading knowledge about a very wide variety of STEM topics. Additionally, we conduct other activities such as educational drives, interviews, and causes.
